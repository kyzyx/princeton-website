B.S.E. Computer Science

I'm an undergraduate at Princeton University majoring in Computer Science. I'm also an active musician and wannabe composer.

I've done work in computer music and HCI; recently I've become interested in augmented reality and the HCI aspects of 3D interfaces. I'm also an avid algorithmist, enjoying the challenges of ProjectEuler and the ACM ICPC. I was a competitor at the 2011 ACM ICPC World Finals in Florida.

I'm very active in Princeton's Computer Science community, as a member of the Undergraduate CS Council as well as the President of the Princeton ACM Student Chapter
